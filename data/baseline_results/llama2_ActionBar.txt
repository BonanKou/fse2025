**Functionality**

* Provides a visual structure and interactive elements that are familiar to users.
* Offers a dedicated space for giving an app an identity and indicating the user's location in the app.
* Provides access to important actions in a predictable way, such as search.
* Supports navigation and view switching (with tabs or drop-down lists).
* Supports contextual menus or action modes, which can be invoked by calling startActionMode(ActionMode.Callback) on an Activity instance.
* Provides an interface, ActionMode.Callback, to handle actions performed in the action mode.
